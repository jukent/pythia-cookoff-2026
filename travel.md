# Travel Information

## Location and Lodging

The 2026 Pythia Cook-off will be hosted at the NCAR Mesa Lab:

1850 Table Mesa Dr.
Boulder, CO 80305

The recommended hotel is the [Fairfield Inn](https://www.marriott.com/event-reservations/reservation-link.mi?guestreslink2=true&id=1770417345956&key=GRP).

If you need additional guidance on hotels, reach out to CISL_Events@UCAR.edu.
